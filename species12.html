Loggerhead Sea Turtle

The Loggerhead Sea Turtle (Caretta caretta) is a species of sea turtle known for its large head and powerful jaws. They are found in oceans around the world and are known for their nesting habits on sandy beaches.

Preventive Measures

  • Nesting Beach Protection: Advocate for the protection and preservation of nesting beaches where Loggerhead Sea Turtles lay their eggs.
  • Reduced Artificial Lighting: Minimize artificial lighting on nesting beaches during the nesting season to prevent hatchling disorientation.
  • Marine Debris Cleanup: Participate in marine debris cleanup efforts to remove plastics and other pollutants from the oceans, which pose a threat to sea turtles.
  • Bycatch Reduction: Promote responsible fishing practices to reduce bycatch and protect Loggerhead Sea Turtles.
  • Educational Outreach: Support educational programs and outreach efforts to raise awareness about Loggerhead Sea Turtles and their conservation.
