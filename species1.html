Blue Whale

The Blue Whale (Balaenoptera musculus) is the largest animal on Earth. They are marine mammals that can grow up to 100 feet in length and weigh as much as 200 tons.

Preventive Measures

  1. Support conservation efforts to protect the Blue Whale and its habitat.
  2. Avoid littering and plastic pollution in the ocean to help maintain a clean environment for marine life.
  3. Follow responsible whale-watching guidelines to minimize disturbance to Blue Whales in their natural habitat.
