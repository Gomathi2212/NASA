Indian Ocean

The Indian Ocean is the third-largest ocean in the world, covering approximately 20% of the Earth's surface.

Pacific Ocean

The Pacific Ocean is the largest and deepest ocean on Earth, covering more than 30% of the planet's surface.

Southern Ocean

The Southern Ocean surrounds Antarctica and is the smallest and youngest of the world's oceans.

Atlantic Ocean

The Atlantic Ocean is the second-largest ocean in the world, separating the Americas from Europe and Africa.

Arctic Ocean

The Arctic Ocean is the smallest and shallowest of the world's oceans, located in the Arctic region.
